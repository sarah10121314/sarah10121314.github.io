Return an item

Hi, I’d like to return this dress.

May I ask why you are returning it?

The stitches are coming off and it’s not my size. You do have the free-return policy, right?

I’m sorry for the unpleasant shopping experience. Our policy is that the item can be returned within 7 days from the day you purchased it.

Would you like to exchange it for another one?

No, I’d rather return it.

No problem. May I have your receipt please?

Here you are.

OK, one moment, please. Ma’am, I need you to sign here. Here is your refund. As an apology for any inconvenience and thank you for being the continued customer. Please accept this ten dollars coupon for your next purchase. Is there anything I can help you with?

Looking for a laptop

Hi, what can I do for you?

I’m looking for a new laptop for college. Do you have any recommendations?

Sure, what’s your major?

Computer science. I have a lot of programming courses. And I also like playing computer games.

How about this one? It has 16 gigabytes of RAM, a Nvidia graphic card, and 256 gigabytes of Hard drive storage.

What processor does it have?

Intel core i9 processor. It’s incredibly fast. And it also has a 15-inch LED screen.

Sounds it like has everything. The original price is 12000 , but we do have a 20% discount for newly admitted college students.

That’s way out of my budget. Could you show me something around 8000?
