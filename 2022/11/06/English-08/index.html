08-Rent an apartment && about quarantine

Rent an apartment

Hi, good morning. This is David calling. May I speak to Mr. Stevens?

Yes, this is he. How can I help you?

I saw your ad on the website that you have a vacant place at 152 base street. Is it still available?

Yes, it’s an apartment with two bedrooms, a kitchen and a bath room. Rent is a thousand dollars per month.

Do you take in students?

No problems. As long as you guys keep it clean and neat.

Would you like to take a look at the apartment?

That would be nice. Is tomorrow morning at 9 am good for you?

I can do that. See you tomorrow.

About quarantine

Where is James?

He is in home quarantine. He went to downtown last weekend and now it’s one of high-risk areas. According to the news, there are over ten confirmed cases in the whole city.

Oh, god. Is he close contact?

I guess not. Otherwise he would be placed in the quarantine center. For now he just need to stay at home for several days.

Poor James. He has to work from home.

Well, I kinda like working from home. I can have flexible working hours, plus, I could cut out some commuting time.

That’s true. I hate the morning rush time.

By the way, did you take the Covid test today?
